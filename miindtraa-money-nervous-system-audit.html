The Solar Plexus Money Reset

  1. Place both hands on your solar plexus — the area above your navel, below your chest.
  2. Notice what's there. Tightness, heaviness, numbness, nothing — all of it is data.
  3. Inhale slowly through the nose for 4 counts. Imagine gentle warmth entering that area.
  4. Exhale slowly through the mouth for 8 counts. Let the area soften — even a little.
  5. Whisper, softly: "I am allowed to earn. I am allowed to keep it."
  6. Repeat for three rounds. Especially before quoting fees, sending an invoice, or any money conversation.
